About My Fitness Pal

My Fitness Pal is a comprehensive fitness tracking application that helps you monitor your calorie intake and physical activities. With our user-friendly interface, you can easily log your meals and workouts, set calorie goals, and track your progress towards a healthier lifestyle.

Key Features

  • Log your meals and track calorie intake
  • Set daily calorie goals
  • Log your physical activities and track calories burned
  • View your meal and activity history
  • Integrate with the Nutritionix API for accurate calorie information
